Vin Diesel takes on one of his toughest characters yet in Bloodshot
In the new movie based on the bestselling comic book, Diesel stars as Ray Garrison, a soldier who has been killed and bought back to life by the RST corporation. They have also erased all his memories and killed his wife. With nanotechnology coursing through his veins and an ability to heal himself, Ray is tasked with carrying out lethal missions for the corporation. But as he starts to piece together his former life, he’s on a mission of his own to avenge his wife’s death.
